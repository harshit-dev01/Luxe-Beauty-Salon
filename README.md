# Luxe Salon – Luxury Beauty & Hair Studio Website

## Overview

Luxe Salon is a modern and responsive salon website prototype designed to represent a premium beauty and hair studio. The project showcases a clean user interface, elegant design, and essential business sections commonly found in professional salon websites.

This website was created as part of an AI Website Generation project to explore the practical application of AI-assisted web development and modern front-end design practices.

---

## Features

* Responsive and mobile-friendly design
* Modern navigation bar
* Full-screen hero section
* About Us section
* Services showcase
* Image gallery
* Expert team profiles
* Client testimonials
* Business statistics section
* Appointment booking form
* Professional footer with contact information
* Smooth scrolling and interactive effects using JavaScript

---

## Website Sections

### Home

A visually engaging hero section introducing the salon and its services.

### About Us

Information about the salon, its mission, and commitment to providing exceptional beauty and wellness experiences.

### Services

A showcase of the salon's core offerings, including:

* Hair Styling
* Hair Coloring
* Facial Treatments
* Bridal Makeup

### Gallery

A collection of salon-related visuals that highlight the salon environment and beauty services.

### Our Experts

Meet the experienced professionals behind Luxe Salon.

### Testimonials

Client feedback and reviews demonstrating customer satisfaction.

### Contact & Appointments

A simple appointment booking form allowing visitors to connect with the salon.
